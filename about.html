How to Play ?


Destiny's dice


First, the user needs to enter his/her name(First name without space) and then the target score to play against the Computer and hit the Play Now ! button



In this game there are two players , one computer and another the user,now user has to ROLL the dice and the number which comes up will be shown as the current score and will be added to the Player's score.



If the number that comes up is 1 your current score will be set to 0, and the turn will pass on to the computer and computer's score randomly which will be shown once you click the Roll button and again the user can take the turn to roll the dice, Otherwise if the number that comes up is between 2 to 6 it gets added to the players score



Note-Inorder to go back to main page from any page click on the "Destiny's Dice"

The Winning Rule:


Player's and Computer's total score will be shown up at the top.
Between the Player and the computer whosoever scores the target score first wins!!
